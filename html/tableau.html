MY TABLEAU VISUALIZATIONS

    

Over the course of the semester I have developed data visualization skills in Tableau. To highlight my aqcuired skills, I've created a set of 4 graphs that seek to visualize the following:

     
         
  • How are profits faring in each of the 50 states?
    •         
  • How has one of the highest-profit states fared for the last two years by profit, shown by month?
    •         
  • How are profits within that state doing for each product category?
    •         
  • For the most profitable category, forecast sales (pick a state that has enough data for a forecast) through November 2015.
    •      
   

1.) Sum profits by state, with the highest profits being found in California.

 
    

2.) California profits from January 2013 - December 2014. Profits show an overall growth.

        
     

3.) California sum proft by category. Office supplies proves most profitable.

        
     

4.) Forcast for office supplies in California looking forward two years. Expecting continual growth.
